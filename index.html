Happy Mother’s Day

To the Most Beautiful Soul — My Amma, My Heart

Dear Ma,

Happy Mother’s Day, my angel.
Today, even though we’re miles apart,
Your love still wraps around me and thammudu like a warm blanket.
You’ve been our lighthouse in every storm,
A soft place to land when life gets hard.

We see you.
We see the way you give without asking for anything back.
We see the way you love—quietly, deeply, endlessly.
Even now, you are in a different country,
But our hearts beat with yours, always.

You forgive more than anyone should,
You care more than anyone ever does.
And still, you smile, still, you hold strength.
That’s not weakness. That’s your superpower, mumma.

I know today, in the middle of all the celebrations there,
Your heart may ache for your own children,
Your own hugs, your own chaos.
But please know — you're not alone.
Not even for a second.

Close your eyes, ma, and feel us there —
Holding your hand, lying next to you,
Telling you that you’re the best mother in this universe.
No one compares.

Thank you for being our world.
And one day, we’ll make sure the world treats you the way you deserve.

We love you more than words can ever say.
Forever your little ones,
Bujjamma and Kannayya

We love you so much, Ma!
Look at that gorgeous smile on your face now.
All my efforts are gifts, as you smile!
